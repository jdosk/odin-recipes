Buffalo Chicken Spice Pizza

Cheesy. Spicy. Delicious.

This pizza takes buffalo chicken to the next level. It adds several ingredients to add a depth of flavor beyond just standard buffalo chicken. Creamy burrata cheese gives the pizza a nice balance in texture while muting the heat, and minced garlic and Blue cheese give the pizza a little zing.

  • Trader Joe's Ready to Bake Pizza Dough
  • Corn meal, or Flour
  • Buffalo Sauce
  • 4 Raw Chicken Tenders
  • Shredded Mozzarella Cheese
  • Blue Cheese Crumbles
  • Burrata Chesse
  • 2 Cloves of Garlic, minced
  • 1 Tsp. of Chili Powder
  • Pinch of Salt and Pepper
  • Olive Oil
  • The Secret Spice
  1. Using corn meal (or flour) to keep from sticking, roll dough into a circle on top of a pizza stone.
  2. Pre-heat oven to 435 degrees.
  3. Dry off chicken with paper towels, and then dice into 1/4 inch cubes. Sprinkle with salt, pepper, and chili powder. Heat a pan of olive oil to medium heat. Add chicken and stir occasionally for 4 minutes. Turn off heat and add 1 Tablespoon of buffalo sauce. Mix and set aside to cool.
  4. Pour 3 Tablespoons of Buffalo Sauce onto dough and spread with the bottom of a large spoon. Sprinkle desired amount of Mozzarella cheese equally accross pizza.
  5. Pull apart burrata cheese and place on pizza, trying to space apart as equally as possible. Sprinkle minced garlic onto pizza.
  6. Dice the cooked chicken one more time. Sprinkle chicken onto pizza.
  7. Sprinkle Blue Cheese, The Secret Spice, and extra Mozzarella, if desired.
  8. Place pizza on middle rack in oven and cook for 15 minutes. Let cool 5-10 minutes before slicing and serving.
