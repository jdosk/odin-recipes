Grilled Cheese

A delicious and easy comfort meal.

  • 2 slices of thick white or sourdough bread
  • 2 Slices of Gruyere Cheese
  • 1 Slice of Sharp Cheddar Cheese
  • Butter
  1. Melt 1 Tbs of butter in a pan on medium heat. Spread butter on 1 side of each of the slices of bread.
  2. Place 1 slice of bread into the pan, butter side down. Place all slices of cheese on the bread.
  3. Place remaining bread slice on top of the cheese, butter side up.
  4. Let cook for 2-3 minutes, or until bottom of the sandwhich is browned.
  5. Carefully flip the sandwich using a spatula.
  6. Cook for another 2-3 minutes until the other side is also browned.
  7. Remove from pan and let cool for 1-2 minutes before eating.
