Shepards Pie

A delicious comfort meal for a cold night.

  • 2 Lbs of Ground Lamb or Beef (preferably 1lb of each)
  • 1 Yellow onion, grated (keep the liquid from grating)
  • 2 Medium Carrots, grated
  • 1 1/2 cup of Red Wine
  • 1 1/2 cup of Beef or Chicken stock
  • 1 Tbs of Tomato Paste
  • 3 cloves of Garlic, minced
  • 1 Tbs of olive oil
  • 1 tsp of dried Rosemary (or to taste)
  • 1 tsp of dried Thyme (or to taste)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Mashed potatos (instant are fine, homemade will work better)
  1. Pour olive oil into pan on medium heat, and add lamb/beef to brown.
  2. Once lamb/beef has browned, add a pinch of salt and pepper, and all of the carrots and onions (including juice from onion)
  3. Stir mixture and let onions cook for 1 minute. Add garlic and cook for 1 more minute.
  4. Add tomato paste and stir and let cook for 1 more minute. Add rosemary and thyme, and another pinch of salt and pepper.
  5. Pour in red wine and let simmer until liquid is nearly cooked off.
  6. Pour in beef stock and let simmer for no more than 2-3 minutes.
  7. Remove from heat and scoop mixture into casserole dish. Scoop mashed potatoes onto mixture so that it is completely covered. Bake for 12 minutes at 400F.
  8. Remove from oven and let cool for 5 minutes before serving.
