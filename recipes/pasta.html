Pasta Bolognese

A nice savory pasta

  • 1 lb of Ground Beef (90 / 10)
  • 1 lb of Ground Lamb
  • 1/2 lb of Ground Pork
  • 2 medium Yellow onion, finely chopped
  • 4-6 Stalks of Celery, finely chopped
  • 2 medium Carrots, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves of Garlic, minced
  • 1 32 oz can of Tomato sauce
  • 2 Tbs Butter
  • 1 1/2 cups of Milk
  • 2 cups of White Wine
  • 1/4 tsp of Nutmeg
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 pinch of Cayenne pepper
  1. Melt butter in a large pot on medium heat. Once melted add onions, carrots, and celery. Cook until translucent.
  2. Add beef, lamb, and pork and cook until browned, stiring occasionally.
  3. Once browned, add garlic and cook for 1 minute. Add nutmeg, salt, pepper, and cayenne pepper.
  4. Stir in milk and let simmer until liquid is mostly (but not completely) reduced.
  5. Stir in wine and let simmer until liquid is mostly (but not completely) reduced.
  6. Add tomato sauce, then refill can with water and add. Mix well then let simmer for 4-6 hours.
  7. While simmering, occasionally skim the fat off the top. If liquid reduces too quickly add water or chicken stock as needed.
  8. Once sauce has simmered for at least 4 hours and has reached desired consisency, serve with rigatoni or your prefered noodles.
